# User Research Summary

## Insights from Weeks 1-3
Our discovery phase focused on how users currently plan and execute gatherings.

### Key Insights
1. **High Cognitive Load:** Users spend hours researching, planning, and orchestrating hosting across Instacart, Vivino, local florists, and specialty shops.
2. **The Mission Precedes the App:** The intent (the "mission") exists before the app is opened. Today, users have to manually translate their mission into individual store orders.
3. **Desire for Delegation:** Users want to delegate the orchestration, not just the delivery. They want a service that understands the event and curates the perfect items from multiple trusted local merchants.
