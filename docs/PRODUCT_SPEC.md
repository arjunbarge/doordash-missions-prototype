# Product Specification

## Vision
DoorDash exists to grow local economies. We extend that mission by becoming the orchestrator, not just the delivery layer, that turns the things people are trying to accomplish into completed missions, powered by the local businesses that do them best.

## MVE Scope
The smallest version that lets users declare a party mission, see a curated multi-merchant cart, swap items intelligently if something is out of stock, and tell us whether the mission worked.

## Anchor Mission
**The Saturday dinner party.** A user is hosting six guests Saturday evening, the boss is coming, the user wants to look like they have it together without spending the day running errands.

## The Six Features
1. Mission-First Home
2. Mission Templates
3. Smart Mission Cart (Multi-merchant)
4. Mission + Item Feedback
5. Mission-Aware Substitutions
6. Mission Memory

## What We Said No To
- ML-based predictive mission inference
- Conversational AI concierge
- Voice-driven mission entry
- Merchant-facing trust dashboards
