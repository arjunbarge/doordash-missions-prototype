# Design System

## Brand Colors
- **Primary Brand:** DoorDash Red `#EB1700`
- **Background:** Warm off-white `#FFFAF6`
- **Text:** Deep charcoal `#1A1A1A`
- **Accent:** Warm gold `#F5C518`

## Typography
- **Font Family:** Inter (matches DoorDash clean, modern aesthetic)
- Headers are hospitable and conversational.

## Spacing & Components
- Generous padding and large tap targets (minimum 44px).
- Warm, rounded corners (12px - 16px radius).
- Soft glows and transitions for interactive elements.
